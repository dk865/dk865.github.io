Welcome to moon-buggy!

Drive a car across the moon in this text-mode video game. Moon-buggy is a simple character graphics game where you drive some kind of car across the moon's surface. Fortunately, your car can jump over dangerous craters!

Supported Architectures: iOS & Rootless iOS
Supported Versions: All, just untested.
Instructions: You need a terminal installed. To run, just type "moon-buggy"
Dependencies: libiosexec1 (>= 1.2.2), libncursesw6
